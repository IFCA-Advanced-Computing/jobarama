Servicio Santander Supercomputacion

Servicio Santander Supercomputación (SSC) makes available to researchers at the University of Cantabria and Spain High Performance Computing resources, both for processing time, networking and storage. SSC is capable of tackling complex simulations or process large volumes of data.
